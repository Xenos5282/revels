About Us
Greetings! Today's generation is one that settles for nothing but the best. The avenues for success for this resilient and driven youth are endless. With this in mind, we present to you Revels'19—Excelsior: A Quest for Excellence.
Revels, MIT Manipal's annual Cultural and Sports festival, is an arena for a complete sense of holistic learning— a space for talents to delve deeper into everything from sports to the fine arts.
Excelsior, meaning 'onward’ and ‘upward,’ complements the logo—the Phoenix, a mythological creature that burns completely and is reborn from its ashes. Revels’19 will stand for the sublime art of achieving excellence and climbing to greater heights.
Join us this March, from the 6th to the 9th, for what promises to be the best four days of your life!
Regards,
Team Revels
